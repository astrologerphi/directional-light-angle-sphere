WebGPU Figure-8 Demo

Directional Light Angle Sphere

A lightweight visualization of a drifting light vector on a translucent sphere.

What you are seeing

The direction of a virtual light source drifts across the sphere following a lemniscate (figure-8) path. A glowing head and fading trail help illustrate recency.

Controls

Use the toggle button to pause or resume the animation. On unsupported browsers a compatibility notice appears instead of the canvas.
